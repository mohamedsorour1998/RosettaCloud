Who We Are

RosettaCloud is an educational technology company founded in Cairo, Egypt, with a mission to make quality education accessible to everyone in the Middle East and North Africa. Since our founding in 2015, we've grown to become a leading platform for online learning in the region.

Our name was inspired by the Rosetta Stone, which unlocked the understanding of Egyptian hieroglyphics and connected ancient knowledge with the modern world. Similarly, we aim to unlock potential and bridge educational gaps across languages and cultures.

Today, we serve students and professionals from across 25+ countries with courses in Arabic, English, and French, taught by industry-leading experts and renowned academics. With our innovative learning platform, we're redefining education for the digital age.
